
| **KPI**                             | **Business Insight**                                              | **Possible Business Action**                                                    |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Total Revenue**                   | Shows overall business performance and growth.                    | Increase revenue through promotions, cross-selling, and expansion.              |
| **Total Orders**                    | Indicates customer demand and sales volume.                       | Improve marketing if order volume declines.                                     |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)**       | Measures how much customers spend per order.                      | Introduce bundles, combo offers, and upselling to increase AOV.                 |
| **Total Quantity Sold**             | Identifies product demand.                                        | Maintain inventory for high-demand products.                                    |
| **Cancellation Rate**               | Reveals operational or customer experience issues.                | Improve inventory accuracy, product information, and delivery timelines.        |
| **Return Rate**                     | Indicates product quality or customer satisfaction.               | Review products with high return rates and improve quality or descriptions.     |
| **Delivery Rate**                   | Measures logistics performance.                                   | Strengthen courier partnerships and optimize fulfillment.                       |
| **Cancelled Revenue**               | Shows direct revenue lost due to cancellations.                   | Focus on reducing cancellations to recover lost sales.                          |
| **B2B Revenue**                     | Shows contribution from business customers.                       | Develop exclusive pricing and services for B2B clients if contribution is high. |
| **B2C Revenue**                     | Shows dependence on individual consumers.                         | Launch personalized marketing campaigns and loyalty programs.                   |
| **Top Selling Category**            | Identifies the products driving the business.                     | Increase stock, advertising, and product variety in these categories.           |
| **Top Selling State**               | Reveals the strongest geographical markets.                       | Open regional warehouses and invest more in marketing there.                    |
| **Top Selling City**                | Highlights cities with the highest demand.                        | Run city-specific promotions and improve local logistics.                       |
| **Promotion Usage Rate**            | Measures the effectiveness of discounts and offers.               | Continue successful promotions and discontinue ineffective ones.                |
| **Average Quantity per Order**      | Shows purchasing behavior.                                        | Encourage bulk purchases with volume discounts.                                 |
| **Most Preferred Shipping Service** | Indicates customer shipping preferences.                          | Improve and promote the most-used shipping option.                              |
| **Customer Type (B2B vs B2C)**      | Helps understand the customer mix.                                | Design separate strategies for businesses and individual customers.             |
| **Order Success Rate**              | Measures operational efficiency from order placement to delivery. | Improve fulfillment processes to maximize successful deliveries.                |
